on december 27, three days before the musical "cats" closes after a record run on broadway, there will be a unique and extraordinary performance, a special production of "cats" where the cats are actually played by cats. a one-night special guest appearance of performers from budapest famed trained hungarian cat circus with tiggery-a, a genuine bengal tiger in the role of old deuteronomy. new york times theater critic blake hammerstein writes that she's anxious to witness this unprecedented example of the theater returning to its primal roots and making amends for centuries of exclusionary focus on the dominant human-centric culture.
